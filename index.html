Conference Highlights

  • Focus on intelligent automotive systems and future mobility, covering four core sub - themes.
  • Invite industry leaders from Tesla, CATL, Waymo, and scholars from MIT/Stanford as keynote speakers.
  • Include diverse activities such as technical reports, workshops, enterprise visits, and social dinners.
  • Adopt a double - blind review mechanism and set up awards for the best paper and the best student paper.

Conference Information

Academic Preparation

  • Main Theme: Automotive Systems and Intelligent Transport
  • Sub - themes: Intelligent Connected Energy - efficient Vehicles, Intelligent Battery Systems, Hybrid Energy Management, End - to - End Collaborative Autonomous Driving
  • Science Committee: Invite IEEE Fellows and editors - in - chief of top journals as chairs.

Schedule

The 3 - day agenda includes the opening ceremony, keynote speeches, technical sessions, workshops, enterprise visits, and social activities.

Highlight on the first day: A speech by a Tesla executive on "The Future Trends of Automotive Intelligence" and a sharing by the director of a CATL laboratory on battery technology breakthroughs.
